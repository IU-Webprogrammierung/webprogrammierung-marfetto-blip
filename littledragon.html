The Little Dragon

The little dragon is an interactive, web-based platform designed to engage primary school children in grades 1 to 4 with mathematics through an adventurous storyline and collaborative tasks. It integrates gamification elements and an intelligent tutor to motivate students while supporting both individual and group learning, emphasizing social interaction and teacher moderation. The solution combines synchronous classroom use with asynchronous home practice, fostering media competence, motivation, and cooperative learning in a child-friendly digital environment.
