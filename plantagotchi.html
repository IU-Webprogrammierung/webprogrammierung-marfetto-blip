Plantagotchi

Platangotchi is a concept for botanical education for children. It transforms houseplants into lively digital companions. Using a smart sensor stick and augmented reality (AR), plants come to life, showing their condition and emotions. From seedling to full growth, users experience the entire growth process interactively and engage in exciting challenges within the Plantagotchi community.
