Surf Guard

Surf Guard combines a smart app with an innovative armband that tracks UV exposure in real-time and features an integrated, water-resistant sun powder. Before surfers get sunburned, the armband alerts them to reapply sun protection. Thanks to the built-in powder brush, surfers can protect their skin without interrupting their session or leaving the water.
